About the Book

I carried you in my heart is a lyrical celebration of the love that leads to adoption. Told with warmth and honesty, the story reveals how parents' hope, longing, and joy all grew into one extraordinary truth: their child was always meant for them. This beautifully illustrated picture book gives adoptive families a meaningful way to share their journey and remind children that they were cherished from the start.

About the Author

Dr. Rori Martello

Rori Martello is a New Yorkbased mom, literacy specialist, & educator with nearly twenty years of experience working with children, teachers, and families. Drawing from her own adoption journey, she wrote I Carried You in My Heart to provide a gentle, loving resource for children, parents, teachers, and caregivers. This is her debut picture book.
